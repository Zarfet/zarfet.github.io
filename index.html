13 years, two disciplines, one perspective

My path into Quality Assurance came through curiosity about how systems work. A background in UX design gave me something most QA engineers don't have: a frame for understanding not just if something works, but whether it makes sense for the person using it.

I've worked across fintech, e-commerce, media, aviation, and telecommunications, among other industries, building test strategies and automation frameworks within Agile/Scrum environments. I establish QA processes from project kickoff, coordinate distributed teams, and mentor junior engineers.

Currently based in Barcelona, completing a Master's in Advanced Design Studies at the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya.
