# WHAT THE FUTURE! - SCENARIO CARDS

---

## SCENARIO 1: POST-PRIVACY ECONOMY

All data tracked and sold.
Social credit controls access.
Data = money.

**Challenge:** Give people control over their data while keeping services running.

---

## SCENARIO 2: BLACKOUT SOCIETY

Power outages daily.
Electricity unreliable.
Infrastructure failing.

**Challenge:** Deliver food without reliable electricity.

---

## SCENARIO 3: MIGRANT METROPOLIS

2 million climate refugees arrived.
Cities overwhelmed.
Housing crisis.

**Challenge:** House 2 million refugees in 6 months.

---

## SCENARIO 4: REWILDED CITY

Nature reclaimed downtown.
Wildlife everywhere.
Infrastructure crumbling.

**Challenge:** Share urban space with wildlife without human casualties.

---

## SCENARIO 5: LONGEVITY & ELDERCORE

Everyone lives past 100.
65+ majority.
Youth scarce.

**Challenge:** Create jobs for 80-year-olds still working.

---

## SCENARIO 6: CRITICAL MATERIALS SCARCITY

Rare earth elements gone.
Tech production stopped.
Recycling mandatory.

**Challenge:** Build communication systems without new rare earth metals.

---

## SCENARIO 7: ALGORITHMIC GOVERNANCE

AI makes all decisions.
Humans can't override.
Efficiency maximized.

**Challenge:** Keep democracy alive when algorithms govern everything.

## SCENARIO 8: COMMONS COLLAPSE

Private property abolished.
Everything shared.
Tragedy of commons.

**Challenge:** Stop resource depletion when nobody owns anything.

Robots took 60% of jobs.

Mass idleness and despair.

## SCENARIO 10: SYNTHETIC FOOD ECONOMY

Natural food is luxury.
99% eat lab-grown protein.
