# WHAT THE FUTURE! - TOOL CARDS

---

## TOOL 1: COMMUNITY CO-DESIGN

People design solutions together.
Local knowledge drives decisions.

**Use when:** Solutions need community buy-in and local expertise.

---

## TOOL 2: BLOCKCHAIN CONSENSUS

Cryptographic ledger.
No central authority.
Transparent verification.

**Use when:** Trust is broken but coordination is needed.

---

## TOOL 3: LOW-TECH ROBUSTNESS

Simple technology.
Works without electricity.
Easy to repair locally.

**Use when:** Infrastructure is unreliable or tech access is limited.

---

## TOOL 4: REPAIR-AS-SERVICE

Fix instead of replace.
Modular design.
Maintenance networks.

**Use when:** New resources are scarce or sustainability matters.

---

## TOOL 5: MARKET MECHANISMS

Price signals allocate resources.
Competition drives innovation.
Individual choice.

**Use when:** Efficiency and rapid adaptation are priorities.

---

## TOOL 6: GOVERNMENT COORDINATION

Top-down planning.
Regulatory power.
Universal enforcement.

**Use when:** Fast collective action is needed at scale.

---

## TOOL 7: COMMONS & RADICAL SHARING

Shared ownership.
Open access.
Collective stewardship.

**Use when:** Exclusion creates more problems than abundance solves.

---

## TOOL 8: ADAPTIVE SYSTEMS

Self-organizing networks.
Feedback loops.
Emergent solutions.

**Use when:** Problems are too complex for fixed plans.

---

## TOOL 9: GENERATIVE AI

Machine learning creates solutions.
Pattern recognition at scale.
Automation.

**Use when:** Human capacity is overwhelmed or speed is critical.

---

## TOOL 10: PROHIBITION & LIMITS

Ban harmful activities.
Set hard boundaries.
Enforce compliance.

**Use when:** Preventing harm is more important than innovation.
