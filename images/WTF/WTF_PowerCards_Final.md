# WHAT THE FUTURE! - POWER CARDS

---

## POWER CARD 1: POINT SWAP

Swap your last round score with opponent's.

**Timing:** Play immediately after round scoring.

**Example:** You got 15, they got 22 → Now you have 22, they have 15.

---

## POWER CARD 2: SCORE THIEF

Steal half of opponent's last round score (rounded down).

**Timing:** Play immediately after round scoring.

**Example:** They scored 21 → You steal 10 points, they keep 11.

---

## POWER CARD 3: DOUBLE DOWN

Your next round score counts × 2.

**Timing:** Play before next round starts.

**Example:** You score 18 in next round → You get 36 points.

---

## POWER CARD 4: INSURANCE

Minimum score guarantee: 15 points this round.

**Timing:** Play before round starts.

**Example:** You score 12 → Auto-bumped to 15 points.

---

## POWER CARD 5: WILD TOOL

Use any Tool Card next round (not just from your hand).

**Timing:** Play before Tool selection.

**Example:** You can pick "Blockchain" even if opponent has it.

---

## POWER CARD 6: CONSTRAINT BYPASS

Ignore the Constraint next round.

**Timing:** Play after Constraint is revealed.

**Example:** Constraint is "No Electricity" → You can use electricity.

---

## POWER CARD 7: SPY

See opponent's Tool choice before selecting yours.

**Timing:** Play during Tool selection phase.

**Example:** They pick "AI" → You pick "Low-Tech" to counter.

---

## POWER CARD 8: TIME WARP

Get +2 minutes design time next round.

**Timing:** Play before next round starts.

**Example:** You have 7 minutes instead of 5 minutes to design.

---

## POWER CARD 9: SABOTAGE

Opponent loses 1 minute of design time next round.

**Timing:** Play before next round starts.

**Example:** Opponent has 4 minutes instead of 5 minutes to design.

---

## POWER CARD 10: MIRROR

Copy opponent's last round score as your own.

**Timing:** Play immediately after round scoring.

**Example:** They scored 23 → You also get 23 points (doesn't remove theirs).

---

**DECK:** Shuffle all 10 cards. Losing team draws 1 per round.
**MAX HAND:** 2 cards. Discard excess.
**USAGE:** Play 1 card per round maximum.
